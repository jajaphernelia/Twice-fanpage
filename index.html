One in a million Hello we are TWICE!

Twice (Hangul: 트와이스; stylized as TWICE ) is a South Korea-based girl group formed by JYP entertainment through the 2015 reality show Sixteen.
The group is composed of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.

The group will touch people's hearts twice, once through the ears, and once again through the eyes.

–Park Jin Young, Meaning of the group

The group debuted on October 20, 2015, with the mini-album The Story Begins.
Their official lightstick is called
Candy Bong
